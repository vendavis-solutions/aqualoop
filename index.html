Aqualoop — Support

Welcome! This page provides help, FAQs, and links to our policies for the Aqualoop water tracking app.

Contact

  • Email: vendavis.apps@proton.me

Tip: When you contact us, please include the app name (Aqualoop), app version (see Settings → About), your device model, iOS version, and short steps to reproduce the issue.

Quick Help / FAQs

  • My daily amount does not look correct.
    Check your daily goal in Settings and make sure the cup size matches what you usually drink.
  • I don’t receive reminders.
    Please verify that notifications for Aqualoop are allowed in iOS Settings → Notifications and that reminder times are enabled inside the app.
  • Can I reset all data?
    Yes. Use the reset option in Settings to clear all logged entries and start fresh.

Known Issues / Status

We are continuously improving Aqualoop with small updates. If you encounter a bug that is not listed here, feel free to contact us via email so we can investigate.

Data & Privacy

Aqualoop stores your hydration data locally on your device. No personal data is sold or shared.
