Simple daily water tracker for iPhone

Stay in your hydration loop without thinking about it.

Aqualoop makes drinking enough water effortless. Quick logging, gentle reminders and a calm design help you build a healthy daily habit – without noisy gamification.

Download on the App Store Support & contact

Available for: iPhone · iOS 17 or later

Key features
  • Quick logging: Add a glass with one tap from a clean, distraction‑free interface.
  • Smart reminders: Gentle nudges over the day – no spam, no stress.
  • Flexible goals: Set your own daily target in ml or cups.
  • History overview: See your last days at a glance and keep the loop going.
Designed for real life
  • Minimal & calm: No accounts, no ads, no clutter.
  • Privacy‑friendly: Your hydration data stays on your device.
  • Small & fast: Lightweight app, optimised for everyday use.
