Privacy Policy

Aqualoop – simple daily water tracker for iPhone

1. Introduction

Thank you for using Aqualoop. We take your privacy seriously. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

2. Data collection and use

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our servers.

The app works entirely offline. We have no access to your personal information, your location, or your usage behaviour.

3. Data storage

All data you enter into Aqualoop (such as water entries, daily goals, and settings) is stored locally on your device (for example using local app storage such as AsyncStorage).

  • We do not run any servers for this app.
  • We do not sync your data to the cloud.
  • If you delete the app, all of your data is permanently removed from the device.

4. App permissions

The app may request the following permission to work correctly:

  • Notifications: We use local notifications to remind you to drink according to your settings. These notifications are scheduled locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers to trigger these notifications.

5. Third‑party services

We do not use third‑party analytics services (such as Google Analytics or Firebase) and we do not use any advertising networks. We do not track your behaviour inside the app.
